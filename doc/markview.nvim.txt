âœ¨ Features                                             *markview.nvim-features*

Core features,

â—‡ Preview `Markdown`, `HTML`, $LaTeX$, `Typst` & `YAML` within Neovim.
â—‡ Hybrid editing mode! Allowing editing & previewing at the same time.
â—‡ Splitview! Allows editing & previewing side-by-side.
â—‡ `Wrap` support(markdown only, at the moment)! Allows using text wrapping while
  not losing most rendering features!
â—‡ Highly customisable! You can change almost anything using the config!
â—‡ Dynamic `highlight groups` that automatically updates with the colorscheme!
â—‡ `Callout`, `checkbox` completions for `blink.cmp` & `nvim-cmp`.
